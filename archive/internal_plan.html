Weaving Strategy
Into Software.

We identify fragmented workflows in mid-market and enterprise organizations and re-thread them into seamless, data-driven systems. We replace legacy debt with custom, owned solutions—but only after rigorous validation.

Organizational Tenets (DRAFT)

01

Consulting Before Coding

We are strategic partners to $1M+ revenue organizations. Code is a tool, not the product. We value impact over hours.

02

Validate, Then Build

We never build "speculative" products. Mockups sell the vision; engineering starts only after the contract is signed.

03

Data Over Intuition

We quantify the pain (e.g., "$10k/mo lost") before pitching. Research is our spearhead for acquisition.

The 2-Week Sprint Cycle

Phase 1

Intake & Research

Anytime an idea strikes, it goes to the Backlog. Before a Sprint starts, we conduct market research to assess viability.

Week 1

Build & Prep

Monday Sprint Start: Select top backlog item. Create "Demo First" assets. Rebuild prospect's site/app as a high-fidelity mockup.

Week 2

Outreach & Execution

"I built this for you." Cold outreach with assets. Goal: Book demo meetings to validate interest.

Review

Retro & Pivot

Friday Wk 2: Analyze response data. If valid → Contract. If invalid → Kill. Decide topic for Sprint 2.
